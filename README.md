## ⚠️ The Problem Statement
In developing nations like India, the doctor-to-patient ratio is severely strained. While acute hospital care is prioritized, the most vulnerable phase for a patient is often **post-discharge**. 
* **Lack of Monitoring:** Patients go home with complex care routines but no real-time oversight.
* **Communication Barriers:** Language and technical literacy often prevent rural and semi-urban populations from using digital health tools.
* **Delayed Interventions:** Doctors only find out about deteriorating vitals when the patient is readmitted in a critical state.

## 💡 Our Solution & Impact
NextStep Care acts as a digital safety net. By providing a lightweight, low-bandwidth platform, we empower patients to recover confidently at home while giving doctors the actionable data they need to intervene before a crisis occurs. 

## 🗺️ User Journey Workflow

**For the Medical Professional:**
1. **Login & Triage:** Doctor logs in and views the overview dashboard, instantly seeing which patients are marked as "Critical."
2. **Review & Adjust:** Doctor reviews the real-time vital trend charts (Chart.js) and adjusts medication dosages accordingly.
3. **Telemedicine Check-in:** Doctor schedules a virtual consultation for patients requiring visual assessment.

**For the Patient:**
1. **Daily Check-in:** Patient logs into their native-language dashboard (English or Hindi).
2. **Medication Adherence:** Patient marks off their daily prescribed medicine checklist.
3. **Vitals Logging:** Patient inputs their morning blood pressure and blood sugar.
4. **AI Assistance:** If feeling unwell, the patient consults the AI Health Assistant, which flags severe symptoms and recommends immediate professional contact.

## 🎨 UI/UX Philosophy
The application was designed with **Glassmorphism** and a calming blue color palette to reduce patient anxiety. The interface is highly responsive, ensuring a seamless experience whether the user is on a desktop at a clinic or a low-cost mobile device in a remote area.
